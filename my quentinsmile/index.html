Family, Cosmetic & Implant Dentists Located in Brooklyn, NY

Quentin Smile Dental is a modern, family-owned dental practice located in the heart of Brooklyn, NY. We’ve been proudly serving our community for over 25 years when the office started out in a small location on Quentin Rd. In 2016, the father-son duo of Dr.’s Igor and Alexander Khabensky expanded the practice to its new facility on Ocean Avenue.

Today, the dental practice is a state-of-the-art facility which features many advanced technologies from dental lasers to low radiation 3D scanning to 3D printing and intra-oral scanning. That means an approach that focuses on comprehensive treatment, thorough planning, and the highest precision and accuracy of dental treatment.

The philosophy at Quentin Smile Dental is always patient centered. When you walk through our doors, our goal is to be warm and soothing and to deliver the utmost quality and professional service. That’s reflected in small things like cappuccinos while you wait, moisturizing gloves and under-eye patches during treatment, the attention to detail from our doctors and a commitment to your health day in and day out.

Our doctors focus on all areas of dentistry from crown and bridge and restorative dentistry, to oral surgery, endodontics, as well as cosmetic dentistry. The goal of our doctors is not just to fix dental problems, but to identify the root causes. We seek out risk factors and help establish and recommend sound routines and products that are evidence-based in helping our patients maximize the most out of their dental and overall health.

We look forward to meeting you so that you can experience Quentin Smile Dental and all we have to offer. Come meet our wonderful team and we hope that you too become a part of our family.

CALL IT COSMETIC PERIODONTICS

Periodontics is the treatment of teeth and gum diseases and injuries. Cosmetic dentistry is concerned with the aesthetics of your smile. To have a winning smile, you need healthy teeth and gums. With a general dentist at Brooklyn's Family Cosmetic & Implants Dentistry, you can have both. Some of the cosmetic and periodontics procedures available include:

  • Gum disease treatment
  • Tooth extraction, only when necessary
  • Dental crowns, especially after a root canal
  • Crown lengthening
  • Dental bonding
  • Teeth whitening
  • Dental bridges
  • Dental veneers
  • Gum contouring

Additionally, Dr. Khabensky has training in and access to revolutionary laser technology (Biolase WaterLase iPlus), with which they can perform painless laser procedures like laser gum surgery. This dentist is also adept at more traditional oral surgery and can perform a bone graft or gum graft procedure, if needed. Anything you need to maintain your oral health, your general dentists can deliver — even temporomandibular joint dysfunction treatment.
